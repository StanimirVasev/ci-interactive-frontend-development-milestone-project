How to play

PokéDuel offers a Pokémon-themed twist on rock-paper-scissors.

Pick from Water, Fire, or Grass Pokémon types.

Defeat the computer by selecting a type that beats its choice. For example:

  • Water beats Fire
  • Fire beats Grass
  • Grass beats Water

Win by having the stronger Pokémon type; lose if weaker or tie if the types match.

The first to win 10 rounds emerges victorious!
